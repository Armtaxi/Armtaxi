Customer Information

Vehicle Information

  • Any sedan available: Swift Dzire, Toyota Etios, Tata Zest, Hyundai Xcent, Nissan Sunny - one will be assigned
  • Any SUV available: Innova, Xylo, Ertiga - one will be assigned
  • Maximum 4 passenger capacity for Sedan
  • Maximum 7 passenger capacity for SUV

Trip Requirements

  • Minimum 130km coverage for one-way trip
  • Minimum 250km coverage for round trip
  • For Bangalore, minimum 300km per day in a round trip
  • One-day round trip ends by 11pm at your pickup location
  • 1 day means 1 calendar day (from midnight 12 to next midnight 12)

Charges & Policies

  • Waiting charges: Rs. 150 per hour during your trip
  • No waiting charges for food breaks, tea breaks, and restroom stops
  • Cancellation charges: Rs. 300 after the driver has arrived
  • Luggage loading in carrier: Rs. 300 if mentioned in the quotation
  • Permit charges and driver bata are as quoted
  • Toll charges paid through Fastag by the driver
  • Payment via online transfer or cash

About ARMTaxi

At ARM Taxi, we pride ourselves on being Tamil Nadu's premier choice for reliable intercity car rentals. With a diverse fleet comprising sedans like Swift Dzire, Toyota Etios, Tata Zest, Hyundai Xcent, and Nissan Sunny, as well as SUVs including Innova, Xylo, and Ertiga, we ensure that every journey is comfortable and convenient for our customers.

Our vehicles are meticulously maintained to offer a clean and pleasant travel experience. We prioritize customer satisfaction by providing transparent billing, courteous drivers, and attractive rates.

Whether it's a one-way trip with a minimum coverage of 130km or a round trip with a minimum coverage of 250km, we guarantee a seamless journey from start to finish. With flexible payment options, efficient handling of toll charges via Fastag, and clear policies on waiting charges and cancellations, Droptaxi is committed to making your travel hassle-free and enjoyable.
